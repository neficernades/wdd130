Arequipa

Welcome to the thrilling white water rafting trip in Arequipa, Peru! Prepare for an unforgettable adventure as you navigate the turbulent waters of the Chili River canyon, surrounded by breathtaking scenery. Admire the imposing canyon walls and unique flora and fauna as you ride the river's exhilarating currents, creating unforgettable memories of this exciting experience in southern Peru. Whether you're a beginner or an expert rafter, this trip promises a sense of achievement and the opportunity to explore one of Peru's most impressive natural wonders. Join us for an adventure you'll treasure forever!

Lunahuana

Welcome to the thrilling rafting adventure in Lunahuaná, Peru! Immerse yourself in the pristine waters of the Cañete River and prepare for an adrenaline-fueled journey amidst breathtaking scenery. As you navigate downstream, you'll be captivated by the awe-inspiring natural beauty enveloping Lunahuaná, with towering mountains providing a stunning backdrop to your aquatic escapade. Conquer challenging rapids like "El Infiernillo" and "El Laberinto" with our experienced guides ensuring your safety, indulge in refreshing dips, and bid farewell to the river with unforgettable memories of this exhilarating experience. Whether you're a novice or seasoned rafter, this adventure promises a sense of accomplishment and a longing for more, making it an unforgettable experience to treasure forever.

Cusco

Prepare yourself for an exhilarating white water rafting escapade amidst the untamed waters of Cusco, Peru! Embark on a journey down the Urubamba River, traversing through breathtaking Andean landscapes characterized by majestic mountains and picturesque valleys. Conquer thrilling rapids such as "El Resbaladero" and "La Mayolica" with the guidance of our certified experts, ensuring both your safety and maximum enjoyment throughout the adventure. Upon concluding your journey, depart from the river with cherished memories and a profound sense of accomplishment, having partaken in a truly unique experience in Cusco. Whether a novice or seasoned rafter, this expedition promises unforgettable moments and the assurance of exploring one of Peru's most remarkable natural wonders. Embark with us on an adventure destined to linger in your memory forever!
